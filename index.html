Overview

1600 Sqft Condo

* 8 Guest
* 3 Bedrooms
* 3 Bathrooms

This condo is tastefully decorated and has:

  • A wonderful view of a beautiful cove with great views of the water and the changing seasons.
  • Our complex has great amenities that makes this a wonderful year round place to stay.
  • We have an indoor and outdoor pool, steam room, and sauna all in our comfortable club house.
  • Our condo is spacious and is ideal for family vacations, girls weekends, or golf getaways.
  • Master bedroom has a nice California king and a flat screen tv with Spectrum cable and free WIFI throughout.
  • The other two standard bedrooms have Queen beds and flat screen tvs.
  • The lake offers many attractions, shopping, golfing, wineries, as well as outdoor and indoor water parks.
  • The Lake Ozark area has many beautiful walking and hiking trails for everyone to enjoy.
  • Our condo is a mile away from the Margaritaville at the Lake of the Ozarks.
  • Our condo also has everything you need to relax and unwind and enjoy the beauty of the lake.
  • The wood burning fireplace is not to be used at this time.
